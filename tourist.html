Switzerland Tourist Guide

Introduction

Switzerland is a landlocked country in Central Europe. It is known for its stunning mountains, beautiful lakes, and charming towns.

The country is divided into 26 cantons, each with its own unique culture and history.

Switzerland is a popular tourist destination, and for good reason. There is something for everyone in this beautiful country.

Top Tourist Destinations

  • Interlaken
  • Zermatt
  • Jungfraujoch
  • Lucerne
  • Geneva

Things to Do

  • Skiing and snowboarding
  • Hiking and mountaineering
  • Lake swimming
  • City sightseeing
  • Wine tasting
